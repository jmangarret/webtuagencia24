Joom!Fish - be in control of your localization

The Joom!Fish extensions provides you the capabilities to manage, control and maintain your different language versions of your website that go beyond the core Joomla features.
The purpose of this extension is a translation management for all content stored in any extension you use with Joomla. To achieve this you need to follow 4 basic steps:

  1. Define the languages in which you like to allow your users to view your website using the content language manager.
  2. Configure the JoomFish language selector and the JoomFish specific router plugin in the way you like your website to behave.
  3. To translate your information such as menus or articles please use the translation overview.
    After you created the translated (linked) item once you can edit them within the Joomla system directly. Don't forget to publish your translations ;-).
  4. Add additional translation maps (content elements) to allow the translation of additional information such as your shop, calendar and many more.

Managing website content is made really easy using Joomla! CMS. Adding an other language however can increase the complexity quite a lot.
We highly recommend that you plan your website structure in one language first before you start translating the information. Also plan the translation as sometimes you will encounter strange behavior of your site if you changed something in your original but did not updated the translation accordingly.

Our community support forum is a great source and free of charge knowledgebase for you. You will find a lot of information related multilingual websites, localization and more.

Additionally we provide extended support for this extension that is based on various support and service plans. These plans are availble in our and give you additional support during your installation and extended service period.


Joom!Fish extension status

This release is a stable release for the Joomla 2.5.x code base. It is backward compatible with all Joom!Fish 2.x releases in terms of database content.

However due to the massive changes between Joomla 1.5 and 2.5 a migration of your site will re required. This migration can only be supported by additional extensions and supporting functions. All of the migration scripts are not included in this core package!
